ServiceNow Introduction:

ServiceNow is a cloud-based software platform that helps businesses manage operations, improve customer experience, and optimize productivity. It was founded in 2004 by Frederic B. Luddy.
ServiceNow was introduced as a software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform that automates business processes. It uses machine learning to analyze data and workflows to help businesses become more scalable and faster. ServiceNow creates a single system of record for all service management processes within an organization. It offers a broad range of tools and modules to optimize business workflows.
